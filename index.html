Shining Light on Single Spins

Advancing quantum sensing at the nanoscale to unlock new frontiers in science and technology

Welcome to the Stevenson Group

Welcome to the website of the Stevenson group at Northeastern University! We are a highly interdisciplinary lab using spin-based quantum sensors to address important scientific questions spanning chemistry, physics, biology and materials science.

Our research focuses on developing and applying cutting-edge quantum sensing techniques to study fundamental processes at the single-molecule and nanoscale level. By harnessing the unique properties of quantum systems, we push the boundaries of measurement precision and explore phenomena that were previously inaccessible.

Research Areas

Quantum Sensing

Developing novel quantum sensors based on single spins for unprecedented measurement sensitivity and spatial resolution.

Materials Science

Investigating quantum materials and their properties using advanced spectroscopic techniques and spin-based probes.

Biophysics

Applying quantum sensing to study biological systems at the molecular level, revealing new insights into life processes.

Nanoscale Physics

Exploring quantum phenomena at the nanoscale and developing tools for single-molecule detection and manipulation.
